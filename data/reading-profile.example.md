# Reading Profile

Example file. Copy it to `data/reading-profile.md` (or simply start the
agent, it creates an empty template) and enter your own data. The agent
reads `reading-profile.md` again on every iteration. Changes automatically
trigger a fresh scoring of both leaderboards.

## Current reading wish

Bullet points describing what you want to read right now.
Leave empty for general recommendations matching your taste.

- Books about space travel
- Historical novels with a real historical background

## Books read

Books you have read and enjoyed. Type: `nonfiction` or `other`.
They are never proposed again and steer what gets recommended next.

| Title | Author | Type |
|-------|--------|------|
| The Martian | Andy Weir | other |
| A Brief History of Time | Stephen Hawking | nonfiction |
